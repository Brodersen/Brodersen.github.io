Haptic Navigator Privacy Policy

The Haptic Navigator app (the iOS app for iPhone and associated Apple Watch Companion app) does not send any data to the developer or any third parties.

The app only includes Apple provided frameworks and APIs. This is limited to calls to Apple Maps and geo-location services used only during the start of an activate navigation.

Data stored on the users device is limited to finished navigation records and app settings. This data is stored locally on the device and can be viewed and deleted from the History and Settings screens respectively.

If permitted by the user, the app can store finished navigations as Walking Workouts in the users Apple Fitness App. This can be disabled at any time in the app settings or by disallowing the app access to write to the users Health Data. The app is not able to read the users existing health data.

Health and location data is private and personal. Haptic Navigator was made with respect to these values.

This policy is effective as of 2021-08-15
